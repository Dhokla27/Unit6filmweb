Film Production Assistant

Job Description: As a Film Production Assistant, you will support the production team in all stages of filmmaking, from pre-production through post-production. Your responsibilities will include coordinating schedules, managing equipment, assisting with set preparation, and handling administrative tasks. This role requires strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment. Prior experience in film production or a related field is preferred. Key Responsibilities: • Assist in organizing and coordinating film shoots. • Manage and transport production equipment. • Support set preparation and breakdown.{TEXT FROM AI }

Film Editor

Job Description: The Film Editor is responsible for assembling recorded raw material into a finished product suitable for broadcasting. This role involves selecting and combining the best shots, adding music, dialogue, and effects to create a cohesive and engaging story. A successful Film Editor has a keen eye for detail, a deep understanding of narrative structure, and proficiency with editing software such as Adobe Premiere Pro or Final Cut Pro. Key Responsibilities: • Review and organize raw footage. • Collaborate with directors and producers to understand their vision. • Edit video clips, adding transitions, effects, and audio.{ TEXT FROM AI }
